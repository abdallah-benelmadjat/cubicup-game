How to Play

1 Overview

CubiCup is a 2-player strategy game. Each player chooses a color and takes turns placing cubes on a pyramid-shaped board. The goal: be the player who places the final cube at the top.

2 Setup

  • Each player selects a set of colored cubes.
  • The board starts completely empty.
  • Yellow always goes first.

3 Building CubiCups

  • A CubiCup is formed by arranging 3 cubes next to each other.
  • Cubes can belong to multiple CubiCups simultaneously.
  • Place cubes anywhere on the board as long as the placement is valid.

4 Turn Rules

  • Players alternate placing cubes each turn.
  • When you form a CubiCup, your opponent must fill all CubiCups you created.
  • If your opponent then creates CubiCups, you must respond accordingly.

5 Special Rules

  • Placing the last cube at the peak wins the game.
  • If that move completes a CubiCup for your opponent, it may result in a draw.
  • Creating two CubiCups at once can secure a guaranteed win.

6 Tips

  • Try to create multiple CubiCups in a single move.
  • Think ahead — your placement directly shapes your opponent's options.
  • Control the center to maximize future placement possibilities.
